Meet the band

Micky Dolenz

Micky has performed live numerous times, singing the Monkees most popular songs. He has performed on Broadway and published his autobiography.


Michael Nesmith

An accomplished songwriter before performing on The Monkees TV show and has produced movies such as “Tape Heads” and “Timerider”. He still performs live in solo concert shows on occasion.


Davy Jones

A former Broadway performer, Davy was first to be picked for The Monkees TV Show. David performs songs of The Monkees and new material all around the world. He has written books about his time with the Monkees. He also breeds and rides race horses.


Peter Tork

A folk musician before joining the Monkees, Peter played around the world with several bands. He has recently released several albums featuring various collaborators.
